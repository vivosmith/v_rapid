To operate, you can simple enter whatever you are looking for, including a paricular name. Just leave everything but the file name blank and proceed to submit it. 
If you decide you want to apply filters by street (since some people have a last or first name in common with a street or road name in a city), just check the option button. Don't forget to enter the name of the csv file. 
If you want to search by city, choose a state or territory from the drop down menu, enter the file name, and check the city option box.
